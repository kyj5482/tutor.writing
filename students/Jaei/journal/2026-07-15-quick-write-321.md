## Bonus — ACE Write

**Question:** Why do Amy and Dan decide they can't fully trust Nellie anymore — even after everything she's done to help them?

Amy and Dan decided they couldn't trust Nellie because they figured out she was giving away clues to the other team. In the book, Nellie told them that "she had to report to Mr. McIntyre for a status report." This shows that she was giving away the clues that Amy and Dan had worked so hard to find, which was enough to break their trust in her. Some might say that Nellie had done so much for them that they should trust her completely. However, in this clue hunt—where they can become the most powerful people in the world and have risked their lives so many times—it is only right not to trust someone who is giving away all their hard work, even if that person is Nellie.
