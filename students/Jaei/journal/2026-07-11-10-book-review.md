## Tutor feedback

🌟 Two things you did well:
- Your hook is textbook-perfect: "Would you travel half the globe for the 39 clues?" — you opened with a question that pulls the reader right in, exactly like a real book review.
- Your read-alike is specific and smart: "If you liked Keeper of the Lost Cities, you'll love this because it has same amount of action but it is more mystery type." You didn't just name a book — you told the reader HOW it's similar and HOW it's different. That's exactly what makes a recommendation trustworthy.

🔧 One thing to level up (verb check — your focus skill): "It was the plant that **were** growing next to it." The subject is "the plant" — one plant — so the verb should be singular: "the plant that **was** growing next to it." Read it aloud and you can hear it. Try fixing that sentence.
