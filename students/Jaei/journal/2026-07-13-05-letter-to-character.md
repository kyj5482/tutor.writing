## Tutor feedback

🌟 Two things you did well:
1. You reacted to a specific, real line — quoting "we should be glad that [our parents] died" and answering it directly. That's exactly the Tier 3 "quoted line" move.
2. Your reader-knowledge tease — "something horrible will happen, trust me I know" — is a perfect cliffhanger that only you, the reader, can pull off.

🔧 One thing to level up (verb check): "why would you let your anger control yourself" → "control **you**." When the subject and object are the same person you use *yourself*, but here Amy's anger controls *her*, so it's "control you." Read-aloud test catches these.
