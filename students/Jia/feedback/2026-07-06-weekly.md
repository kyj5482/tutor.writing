## 3. Focus for next week — the "So what?"

You've got structure and details down. Your next level-up is **explanation**: after you give your evidence, add a sentence that says *why it matters* — something new, not just saying the same thing again.

Look at your ACE ending from Jul 6:

> "This shows that Harry is seeing things and it is scary for him because Ron can't even see it."

That mostly repeats the answer. Push it one step further to explain the *why underneath*:

> "This shows Harry might feel like something is wrong with **him** — he's scared because there's no one to tell him he isn't imagining it."

See how the second version tells us something we didn't already know? That's the goal: your "This shows…" sentence should **add a new idea**, not restate the old one.
