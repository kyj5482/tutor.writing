## Tutor feedback

🌟 Two things you did well:
- You stayed in "I" as Harry the whole way through — "I started to see things that Ron can't" really sounds like Harry writing in his own diary.
- Your order was perfect: what happened → how you felt → what you hope for tomorrow. That's your Structure focus skill, nailed! 🎯

🔧 One thing to level up: Name the ONE specific thing Harry saw. "Weird thing" leaves your reader guessing — a Tier 1 diary should have one real detail from the book. Try: "Today, I saw something dark and horse-like that Ron couldn't see at all."

🚀 Stretch challenge (+10 XP, optional): Show the feeling in Harry's body instead of telling it. Instead of "I felt like it was weird," try something like "My stomach dropped because I was seeing things Ron can't." That's a Tier 2 move!
