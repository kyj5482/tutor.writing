## Tutor feedback

🌟 Two things you did well:
1. You used real character names — "Alex and Connor" and "grandmother" — instead of just saying "they." That helps the reader know exactly who you're talking about!
2. Your summary has a clear shape: it starts with the characters, moves through the birthday, and ends with the grandmother's arrival and the special book. That's a real beginning–middle–end structure, and that's exactly your focus skill this week!

🔧 One thing to level up:
Your last sentence is doing a lot of work — "Their grandmother bring a lot of presents for them and the book called, Land Of Story, the book that grandmother trvel with." Try splitting it into two sentences so each idea gets its own space. Like this:

*"Their grandmother brought a lot of presents. One of them was a special book called The Land of Stories, which the grandmother had traveled with."*
