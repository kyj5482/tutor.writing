# 08 — Connection (Text-to-Me / Text-to-World)

**Skill:** analysis through personal connection, reflective voice
**Best when:** today's reading reminded you of something — your life, news, another book

## Tier 1 (4–5 sentences)

- In the book, ______ happened.
- This reminds me of ______ (something in my life / the world / another book).
- They are alike because ______.

✅ Checklist: book event + your connection + one "alike because".

## Tier 2 (1 paragraph)

- Describe the book moment AND your connection with **specific details on both sides**
  (when, where, who).
- Then go one step further: how are they **different**?
- Finish: did the connection help you understand the character better? How?

✅ Checklist: details on both sides, one difference, an "I understand them better
because…" sentence.

## Tier 3 (2 paragraphs)

- Connect the book to a **bigger idea** (fairness, survival, fitting in, courage,
  family) — name the idea explicitly.
- Use one short quote from the book as your anchor.
- End with a question the book is really asking, and your honest answer today.

✅ Checklist: named theme, anchor quote, a real question + your answer.
