# 10 — Book Review

**Skill:** evaluation, persuasion, summarizing without spoiling
**Best when:** you FINISHED the book 🎉 (worth +20 XP — see game rules)

## Tier 1 (5–6 sentences)

- The book ______ by ______ is about ______ (no spoilers — just the setup).
- My favorite part was ______ because ______.
- I give it __ out of 5 stars.
- You should read it if you like ______.

✅ Checklist: title + author, a favorite part with because, a star rating.

## Tier 2 (2 paragraphs)

- **Paragraph 1:** hook the reader (start with a question or a bold statement),
  then the no-spoiler setup: who, where, what problem.
- **Paragraph 2:** what the book does well (pacing? funny? characters feel real?) +
  one thing that could be better. Star rating with a reason. Who exactly should
  read it ("If you liked ______, you'll like this").

✅ Checklist: a hook, one strength + one weakness, a "read-alike" recommendation.

## Tier 3 (3 paragraphs)

- Add a paragraph about the book's **big idea** — what is it really about under the
  plot? Use one quote as proof.
- Evaluate one **craft choice** by the author (first-person narration? short chapters?
  cliffhangers?) — did it work?
- Your rating must argue with itself: *It loses a star because ______, but it earns
  ______ because ______.*

✅ Checklist: theme paragraph with quote, craft evaluation, argued rating.
