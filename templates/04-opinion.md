# 04 — Opinion / Response

**Skill:** claim + evidence + explanation (the big one for school essays)
**Best when:** a character made a choice, or something felt unfair/exciting/wrong

Pick a question like: *Did the character make the right choice? Who's really to blame?
Is ______ a good friend? Should ______ have told the truth?* (The tutor will offer one
based on today's reading.)

## Tier 1 (4–5 sentences)

- I think ______ (your opinion).
- One reason is ______ (something from the book).
- That's why I think ______.

✅ Checklist: clear opinion + one reason from the book.

## Tier 2 (1 paragraph)

- **Claim:** state your opinion in one strong sentence.
- **Evidence:** one thing that happened or was said in the book.
- **Explanation:** WHY does that evidence prove your claim? *This shows that ______.*
- **Wrap-up:** restate your claim in different words.

✅ Checklist: has all 4 parts; the explanation is its own sentence, not skipped.

## Tier 3 (2 paragraphs)

- Paragraph 1: claim + **two pieces of evidence** (one as a short embedded quote)
  + explanation for each.
- Paragraph 2: **the other side** — *Some people might say ______, but ______.*
- Close with a sentence that adds a new thought, not a repeat.

✅ Checklist: embedded quote, counterargument addressed, fresh closing line.
