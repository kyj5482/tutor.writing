# 11 — ACE Write (Bonus)

**Skill:** answering a question with text evidence (Answer · Cite · Explain)
**Best when:** the tutor asks ONE question about today's reading *after* the main entry —
this is the optional bonus round.

**ACE = Answer → Cite → Explain**

- **A — Answer** the question directly (turn the question into a statement).
- **C — Cite** proof from the book (a specific detail, or a short quote).
- **E — Explain** how that proof shows your answer is right ("so what?").

## Tier 1 (3–4 sentences)

Fill in the frame:

- **A:** The question asks ______, and I think ______.
- **C:** In the book, ______ (one detail that really happened).
- **E:** This shows ______.

✅ Checklist: you answered the actual question, 1 detail from the book, end punctuation.

## Tier 2 (1 paragraph, 5–7 sentences)

- **A:** Start by turning the question into a clear answer sentence (no "I don't know").
- **C:** Give **one specific detail or a short quote** and say where it happened.
- **E:** Use **2 sentences** to explain how that proof backs up your answer — the *so what*.
- End by tying it back to the question.

✅ Checklist: a real answer sentence, 1 cited detail/quote, an explanation that says *why*
it matters.

## Tier 3 (1 tight paragraph)

- **A:** Open with a **claim** — confident, no "I think".
- **C:** **Embed a short quote with context** (set up who/when, then quote 3–8 words).
- **E:** Explain the **significance**: what it reveals about the character, conflict, or
  theme. Close with a sentence that adds an insight, not just repeats the answer.
- Optional stretch: add an *"Some might say… but…"* to handle the other side.

✅ Checklist: a claim (not a guess), an embedded quote with context, a "why it matters"
closer that goes beyond the question.
