# 07 — Golden Line

**Skill:** close reading, quoting, responding to an author's words
**Best when:** any day — even a few pages. Great for short reading days.

Choose ONE sentence from today's reading that stopped you — funny, beautiful, scary,
true. Copy it exactly, with quotation marks and the page number if you can.

## Tier 1 (quote + 3 sentences)

- **My golden line:** "______" (p. __)
- I picked this line because ______.
- It made me feel ______.

✅ Checklist: quote copied exactly with quotation marks, one real reason.

## Tier 2 (quote + 1 paragraph)

- Copy the line, then answer: What is happening in the story at this moment?
- Why do you think the **author** wrote it this way? (a word choice? short sentence
  for shock? a comparison?)
- What does it make you predict or wonder?

✅ Checklist: context given, one comment about the author's craft, a wonder/prediction.

## Tier 3 (quote + 1–2 paragraphs)

- Zoom into **one word or image** in the line. Why that word and not a plainer one?
- Connect the line to something bigger: the character's whole journey, a theme,
  or your own life.
- Try writing **your own sentence in the same style** about anything you want.

✅ Checklist: single-word zoom-in, bigger connection, your imitation sentence.
