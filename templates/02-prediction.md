# 02 — Prediction

**Skill:** inference, backing ideas with evidence
**Best when:** you stopped at a cliffhanger, or only read a few pages

## Tier 1 (3–4 sentences)

- I think ______ will happen next.
- I think this because in the book, ______.
- If I'm right, then ______.

✅ Checklist: a clear guess + one "because" from the book.

## Tier 2 (1 paragraph)

- **Prediction:** what will happen next — be specific (who, what, where).
- **Two clues:** two things from today's reading that point to it.
  *One clue is ______. Another clue is ______.*
- **Confidence:** how sure are you (1–10) and why?

✅ Checklist: 2 clues that are really in the book, not just "I have a feeling".

## Tier 3 (1–2 paragraphs)

- Make **two competing predictions** (what you *hope* happens vs. what the author
  is *probably* setting up).
- Support each with evidence — include one short quote.
- Explain which one fits the **pattern of the book so far** (Is this author the
  happy-ending type? How do they treat this character?).

✅ Checklist: 2 predictions, 1 quote, reasoning about the author's pattern.
