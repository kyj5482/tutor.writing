# 09 — Quick Write 3-2-1

**Skill:** habit keeper — keeps the streak alive on busy or short-reading days
**Best when:** you only read a few pages, or you're tired (it still counts! +10 XP)

## Tier 1 (lists are fine)

- **3** things that happened (one short sentence each)
- **2** words from the book you liked or didn't know
- **1** question you have

✅ Checklist: all 6 items filled in.

## Tier 2 (full sentences)

- **3** things that happened — full sentences with character names
- **2** new/interesting words **+ your guess of what they mean** from context
- **1** question — and your best guess at the answer

✅ Checklist: full sentences, word-meaning guesses, question + guess.

## Tier 3 (mini-paragraph finish)

- Do Tier 2, then add a **3-sentence "So what?"**: out of everything today, what
  mattered most and why?

✅ Checklist: Tier 2 complete + a 3-sentence "so what" with a *because*.
